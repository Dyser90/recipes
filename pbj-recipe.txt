Peanut-Butter and Jelly Sandwich

Ingredients:
1. Peanut Butter
2. Jelly
3. Bread (at least 2 slices)

Instructions:
1. Spread the Peanut Butter on at least one slice of bread
2. Spread Jelly on at least one slice of bread. 
3. Put slices of bread together. 
4. Slice in half or eat whole. 
5. Enjoy!
